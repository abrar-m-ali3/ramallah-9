About

Ramallah was opened primarily as a sweets shop. After a year of dedicated service, the manager transformed the shop into a restaurant due to his passion for grilling. Ramallah Grille & Sweets still makes and serves its delicious desserts upon request. Ramallah Grille is known for its hospitality, cultural cuisine and especially its succulent, juicy meats bursting with flavor! Come experience it for yourself!
